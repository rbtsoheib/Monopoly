About Monopoly Round World

Monopoly Round World marks an immersive cultural and technical competition where participants embark on a global journey through interactive country stands . From Japan to Egypte, France to Brazil , each destination offers fascinating cultural insights alongside technical challenges that put your problem-solving skills to the test

Scheduled for a single day , this event is an opportunity to explore diverse cultures , demonstrate your tech expertise, and compete with fellow participants. Solve challenges, collect stamps in your personal passport , and earn points for every brilliant solution. Complete your journey and claim your spot as the ultimate “World Traveler” by combining cultural knowledge with technical excellence .!
